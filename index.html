Why it's locked to US + Android

Geo lock

United States only

The advertiser is paying to grow Cash Giraffe's US user base specifically. A US install is what they're paying for; one from another country isn't, so the link won't pay — and often won't even load — outside the States. Traffic from elsewhere just burns clicks.

Device lock

Android only

The bounty is tied to a Google Play install. There's nothing here an iPhone can download, so iOS visitors can never convert. Sending iPhone users to this link is wasted traffic, not a lead.
